Features Developed by Me

Movement System

Implemented a versatile movement system including sprinting, crouching, and walking mechanics. The system is designed to be responsive and intuitive, allowing players to easily navigate through different terrains while managing stamina.

Health System

Developed the health system, incorporating UI elements to visually represent the player's health and utilizing screen effects like damage flashes to provide immediate feedback during combat situations. The health system ensures players are aware of their status at all times.

Interaction/Ammo System

Designed and implemented the interaction and ammo management system, enabling players to collect and manage ammunition and interact with objects in the environment. This includes prompts for interaction and a mechanism for picking up and storing items.

Shooting System

Engineered the shooting mechanics, including bullet physics, recoil, and impact effects. The shooting system is integrated with the ammo system, ensuring realistic ammo consumption and reload logic, enhancing the overall combat experience.

Weapon System

Created a modular weapon system that supports multiple weapon types, allowing players to switch between different firearms seamlessly. This system includes weapon stats, reload animations, and switching logic to offer diverse gameplay styles.
